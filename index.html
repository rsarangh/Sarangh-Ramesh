About Me

Welcome to my website! I'm Sarangh Ramesh KV, and I'm thrilled to share a bit about myself with you. I am currently pursuing an integrated M.tech from Vellore Institute Of Technology, specializing in AI and ML. Throughout my journey, I have developed a diverse skill set that spans various creative disciplines. As an Android developer, I have honed my coding skills and created innovative and user-friendly applications. I am passionate about designing captivating user interfaces and delightful user experiences, ensuring that every interaction with my creations is seamless and enjoyable. Graphic design and video editing are two more areas where I channel my creative energy. I have a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of visual communication, allowing me to craft stunning graphics and videos that convey compelling messages. One of my notable achievements is my proficiency in creating Augmented Reality (AR) effects. I have garnered immense recognition for my work, with my Instagram filters amassing over 1.8 billion impressions. This accomplishment has not only pushed the boundaries of AR technology but has also allowed me to connect with a vast audience and make a lasting impact.

I am also a certified Meta Spark creator, demonstrating my expertise in developing immersive experiences using cutting-edge technologies. By leveraging the power of augmented and virtual reality, I aim to redefine how people interact with the digital world. Beyond the digital realm, I find solace in the world of art. I am an avid sketch artist, where I bring my imagination to life through intricate drawings. Furthermore, I am deeply passionate about photography, capturing moments that tell stories and evoke emotions. My website serves as a platform to showcase my work, share my knowledge and experiences, and connect with like-minded individuals. I invite you to explore my portfolio, dive into my projects, and get in touch if you have any questions or collaboration opportunities. Let's embark on a journey of creativity and innovation together!
